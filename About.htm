This non-profit application offers a unique interactive approach to the Bible for young adults and adults as they face the challenges of just living day to day in this world.

Ask Jesus difficult questions from 28 possible categories:

  • Abuse
  • Bible
  • Christ's Return
  • Creation
  • Fear
  • Forgiveness
  • Guilt
  • Heaven
  • Hell
  • Hope
  • Jealousy
  • Leadership
  • Love
  • My Future
  • My Gifts
  • Partying
  • Prayer
  • Questioning
  • Religion
  • Sad
  • Saved
  • Self Control
  • Sex
  • Sickness
  • Sin
  • Stressed Out
  • Stuck Up
  • Witnessing

Each category has five possible questions with answers to find guidance, encouragement and additional resources to search for Biblical Truth.

A portion of your download will go to further the gospel in areas of Student Missions, Christian Education and Poverty Focused Ministries. You will receive a crown and collect jewels once a day for each time you enter the application and navigates all the way to an answer. The jewels hold a significant meaning as you discover your worth as a child of God.
