This Christian reference application offers a unique interactive approach to the Bible for young adults and adults as they face the challenges of just living day to day in this world.

Ask Jesus difficult questions from 28 possible categories:

  • Abuse
  • Bible
  • Christ's Return
  • Creation
  • Fear
  • Forgiveness
  • Guilt
  • Heaven
  • Hell
  • Hope
  • Jealousy
  • Leadership
  • Love
  • My Future
  • My Gifts
  • Partying
  • Prayer
  • Questioning
  • Religion
  • Sad
  • Saved
  • Self Control
  • Sex
  • Sickness
  • Sin
  • Stressed Out
  • Stuck Up
  • Witnessing

Each category has five possible questions with answers to find guidance, encouragement and additional resources to search for Biblical Truth.

iDialJesus believes in offering support to further the gospel in areas of Student Missions, Christian Youth Education and Poverty Focused Ministries. Christian music plays in the background as you participate in the “Earn a Jewel Game”. You will receive a crown after your download of this application. Then, collect jewels each time you get to the real answers in Christ. Only one jewel will be given per day. It takes 23 jewels to complete your crown. The jewels hold a significant meaning as you discover your worth as a child of God.

Go ahead and Dial Jesus!
